# Intent

Operate an online direct-to-consumer storefront selling specialty single-origin coffee beans. Starting from scratch, grow and sustain a profitable ongoing business — roasting partnerships, inventory, product catalog, pricing, marketing, fulfillment, customer support, analytics, and compliance.

# Criteria

Healthy ongoing operation: orders consistently fulfilled on time, customer satisfaction high, cash flow positive or on a credible path to positive, inventory neither stocking out nor sitting stale, marketing pipeline producing steady new-customer acquisition, support queues under control, and compliance obligations met. No terminal state — the gap is filled moment-to-moment by the aggregate of its open workable children.
