# Intent

Run the content marketing function for a Series-A B2B SaaS company selling observability tooling to engineering teams. Continuously produce and distribute content (blog posts, technical deep-dives, case studies, webinars, newsletters), manage SEO, track channel performance, coordinate with product-marketing on launches, engage developer communities, and maintain a calendar across channels.

# Criteria

Healthy ongoing function: publishing cadence hit across owned channels, organic search traffic trending up or stable-and-high, qualified lead volume from content meeting quarterly targets, content calendar populated at least 4 weeks ahead, SEO technical health in good shape, newsletter engagement rates not degrading, community presence active, brand voice consistent across surfaces. No terminal state — the function is never "done."
