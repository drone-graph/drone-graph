# Intent

Conduct ongoing investment research covering small-cap public and late-stage private companies in the AI infrastructure space (compute, inference, data pipelines, serving, vector databases, observability, agent runtimes). Produce and maintain coverage reports, watchlists, thesis documents, earnings-cycle updates, thematic deep-dives, and quick-takes on news. Track the competitive landscape and flag changes that invalidate prior theses.

# Criteria

Healthy ongoing coverage: watchlist current, each covered name has a live thesis doc updated at least quarterly and after every material news event, earnings-cycle notes published within 72 hours of each relevant release, thematic report shipped at agreed cadence, no known outstanding invalidations of prior theses left unaddressed, sourcing (filings, transcripts, calls, expert networks) logged and fresh. No terminal state — coverage is perpetual.
