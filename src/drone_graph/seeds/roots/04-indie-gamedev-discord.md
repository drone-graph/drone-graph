# Intent

Operate a 50,000-member Discord community for indie game developers. Ongoing responsibilities include moderation (content policy, conflict resolution, ban appeals), community health (events, AMAs, jams, showcase threads), growth (partnerships, invites, cross-community collaborations), new-member onboarding, channel structure evolution as the community grows, bot / automation tooling, and crisis response when drama or bad actors surface.

# Criteria

Healthy ongoing community: day-to-day moderation queue at or near zero backlog, regular events happening and attended, new-member retention above threshold, no unresolved crises, member sentiment positive (surveyed or inferred), growth steady or intentional (not runaway or stagnant), rules and channel structure legible to newcomers, moderator burnout not accumulating. No terminal state.
